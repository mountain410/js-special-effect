1. Live well, love lots, and laugh often.
 善待生活，热爱一切，经常开怀大笑。

2. The best preparation for tomorrow is doing your best today.
 为明天最好的准备就是今天做到最好!

3. Knowledge makes humble, ignorance makes proud.
 博学使人谦逊，无知使人傲慢。

4. Read, study and learn about everything imporant in your life.
 点点滴滴皆重要，处处学习是诀窍。

5. Every man is his own worst enemy.
 每个人最大的敌人是自己。
